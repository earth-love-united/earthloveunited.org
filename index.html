Who we are

A Short Story About Us

Founded in 2020, Earth Love United connects researchers, communities, and buyers around practical nature-based climate work. We pair open evidence with on-the-ground relationships—from restoration research to higher-integrity carbon procurement.

Mission

To drive high-integrity climate solutions through research, education, and inclusive partnerships—harnessing nature, data, and innovation to remove carbon, restore ecosystems, and uplift the communities most affected by climate change.

Vision

To create a just, climate-resilient world where science, innovation, and community action restore balance to the planet and empower every generation to thrive.

Tribute · 1972–2024

Remembering Jean

With profound sadness, we announce the unexpected passing of Jean Missinhoun, founder and chairman of the Earth Love United Foundation, on June 1, 2024, at the age of 52. Jean’s unwavering dedication to environmental preservation has left a lasting mark on our world. Originally from Benin, Jean’s journey led him to London, where he thrived as a hedge fund investor focused on energy investments.

Recognizing the critical crossroads our planet is at, Jean committed to advocating for a shift toward green energy solutions. His book, The Green Energy Boom: A Global Quest for Money, Power and Energy, has inspired countless individuals. Under Jean’s visionary leadership, Earth Love United Foundation forged partnerships with nearly a dozen organizations across the globe and initiated groundbreaking research into sustainable energy sources and natural carbon capture solutions such as hemp, mangroves, seagrass, and algae. His belief in inclusive policies and community empowerment resonates deeply within our ongoing initiatives.

Ekmel Ozdemir, who worked alongside Jean as General Manager since the founding of Earth Love United in 2020, serves as interim chairman. Though we mourn the loss of a visionary leader, we are comforted in knowing that Jean’s spirit will live on through the work of the Foundation. Together, we honor his memory by championing worldwide environmental stewardship and sustainable practices.

Jean’s legacy will be embodied by the advancements of the Foundation but also in the hearts of those he inspired to fight against climate change. We extend our deepest condolences to Jean Missinhoun’s family, friends, and all those whose lives he touched.

May your vision guide us toward a future of harmony with our planet’s precious ecosystems and drive us to never stop saving the world.
