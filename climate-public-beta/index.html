How to read this beta

What the evidence does—and does not—say

01

One frozen factual source

PRIMAP-hist v2.6.1 final is frozen through 2023. This beta displays 2014–2023 harmonized estimates.

02

Not an official inventory

Values are harmonized estimates, not official Party inventory values. They exclude LULUCF and do not include uncertainty bounds.

03

Magnitude is not performance

Any 2023 order compares emissions magnitude among entities using the same metric. It is not a performance, ambition, fairness, or delivery score.

04

Gaps remain visible

No value means no value is available in this release. Gaps are unranked, and missing evidence does not indicate better climate performance.

Climate performance: Not assessed. Commitments and targets are not included in this beta.

    Scope and identity

    Read the boundary before the numbers

    Identity disclosure

    The 249 names and codes come from a Debian-maintained ISO-3166-1-compatible asset. They are not an official ISO or UN M49 publication. Inclusion does not establish sovereignty, UN or UNFCCC status, or eligibility for climate assessment.

    Accounting boundary

    PRIMAP-hist v2.6.1 harmonizes historical emissions. The exact gases, sectors, scenario, unit, GWP convention, transformations, and source hashes appear in each factual series’ evidence details.

    Corrections

    Feedback never edits a live fact directly. Accepted corrections require a new immutable beta release, independent review, and a visible correction-log entry.
